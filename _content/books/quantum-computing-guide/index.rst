Quantum Computing: A Complete Guide
===================================

This guide provides a comprehensive introduction to quantum computing, bridging the gap between quantum physics and computer science. It is designed for readers with a background in computer science or mathematics who want to understand the fundamental principles of quantum computing.

Key Features
------------

- Self-contained introduction to quantum mechanics
- Detailed coverage of quantum algorithms
- Practical examples and exercises
- No prior physics knowledge required

Target Audience
---------------

This book is ideal for:

- Computer scientists interested in quantum computing
- Physicists wanting to understand computational aspects
- Graduate students in related fields
- Researchers exploring quantum algorithms

Prerequisites
-------------

- Basic linear algebra
- Familiarity with complex numbers
- Programming experience (helpful but not required)
- Some knowledge of algorithms and data structures
